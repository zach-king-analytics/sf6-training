# Mental Resilience

## Why this matters for 1600 MR

The biggest hidden MR leak is emotional re-queueing after losses.

## 1300-1400 Pattern

- Queueing immediately after frustration
- Switching game plan without evidence
- Forgetting session focus under pressure

## 1600 Pattern

- Stable between-match reset routine
- Adjustments based on replay evidence
- Strong compliance with post-loss protocol

## Study Prompts

- What tilt signals appear first in my sessions?
- Which situations cause autopilot most often?
- What reset ritual keeps me composed?
