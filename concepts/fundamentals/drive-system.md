# Drive System

## Why this matters for 1600 MR

Drive management is the engine of match control. Poor drive usage creates forced guesses and burnout losses.

## 1300-1400 Pattern

- Overusing Drive Rush without confirming advantage
- Panic Drive Impact in neutral
- Burning out too early in round one

## 1600 Pattern

- Intentional drive budgeting by round state
- DI used as a read, not a panic button
- Parry and movement used to preserve gauge

## Study Prompts

- What situations justify Drive Rush spend?
- Which blocked strings are DI bait traps?
- How often do I enter burnout first?
