# Neutral / Footsies

## Why this matters for 1600 MR

Most MR gains come from cleaner neutral decisions and fewer forced risks.

## 1300-1400 Pattern

- Advancing without spacing checks
- Throwing fireballs at punishable ranges
- Whiffing medium/heavy buttons without buffer discipline

## 1600 Pattern

- Controlled range selection with intention
- Better anti-whiff timing and button economy
- Fireball use tied to opponent rhythm

## Study Prompts

- Which ranges are safe for my key buttons?
- What are my highest-frequency neutral losses?
- Where do I overcommit?
