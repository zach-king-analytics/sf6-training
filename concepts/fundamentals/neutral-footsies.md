# Neutral / Footsies

**Definition:** Managing the space between characters with normal attacks and movement — pressing buttons at the right range and time to win trades, punish whiffs, and control screen position without overcommitting.

## Recognition Cues

- You walked into a poke that is clearly beating you across multiple rounds
- You threw a fireball at a range where it was consistently punished
- You whiffed a heavy normal that lost to their faster button
- You advanced without a read and got counter-hit

## Core Concepts

| Concept | What it means |
|---------|---------------|
| Spacing discipline | Stay at the range where your buttons beat theirs |
| Whiff punish | Wait for a whiffed poke, land a faster punish button |
| Button economy | Only press when you have a reason; idle is valid |
| Range control | Use movement to force opponents out of their comfort range |
| Fireball use | Throw fireball based on opponent rhythm, not random |

## Failure Modes

| Failure | Cause | Correction |
|---------|-------|------------|
| Walking into pokes | No spacing read | Identify their primary poke range; respect it |
| Punishable fireballs | Range blindness | Only throw at ranges where recovery is safe |
| Whiffed heavies | Autopilot button press | Add a reason for every button you press |
| Over-advancing | Impatience | Hold mid range; make them come to you |
