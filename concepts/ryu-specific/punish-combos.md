# Ryu Punish Combos

## Goal

Convert punish opportunities into consistent damage with route selection by resources.

## Route Buckets

- Meterless punish
- Drive Rush punish
- Corner punish route
- Burnout-safe punish

## Common Errors

- Going for optimal routes before consistency exists
- Missed confirms on blocked unsafe moves
- Wrong route for spacing

## Lab Priority

1. Build one high-confidence punish route per bucket.
2. Add one optimized route after baseline consistency.
