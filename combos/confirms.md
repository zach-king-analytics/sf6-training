# Hit Confirms

Combo routes that begin from a poke or check you need to confirm on hit before committing.
Routes are grouped by starter.

---

## Light Starter — Meterless

### cr.LK > cr.LP > cr.MK xx LP Hadouken

**Notation:** `cr.LK > cr.LP > cr.MK xx LP Hadouken`

| Resource cost | Position | Damage (approx) | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 0 bars | Midscreen | ~900 | Not Started |

**Starter:** cr.LK close-range poke or tick throw setup

**Key timing:**
- cr.LK links into cr.LP are strict — do not delay between them
- cr.MK cancel window into Hadouken is lenient, but must be done quickly after the link

**Common drops:**
- cr.LP whiffs (too far from cr.LK hit)
- cr.MK cancel too late, comes out raw

## cr.MK Starter — Drive Rush BnB

### cr.MK ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ HP DP

**Notation:** `cr.MK ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ HP DP`

| Resource cost | Position | Damage (approx) | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 3 bars | Midscreen | ~2200 | Not Started |

**Starter:** cr.MK mid-range footsie poke, confirmed on hit

**Key timing:**
- DR cancel must be buffered during cr.MK recovery — no frame gap
- st.MP after DR: do not rush, let the DR dash finish then press
- cr.HP link timing: relatively lenient after st.MP

**Common drops:**
- DR cancel too late (cr.MK fully recovers before DR input)
- st.MP whiffs because of DR positioning overshoot
- HP DP drops after cr.HP (timing varies by spacing)

## st.MP Starter — Check into DR BnB

### st.MP > cr.MK ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ HP DP

**Notation:** `st.MP > cr.MK ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ HP DP`

| Resource cost | Position | Damage (approx) | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 3 bars | Close range | ~2500 | Not Started |

**Starter:** st.MP close-range check, walk-in pressure or after DR dash-in

**Key timing:**
- st.MP > cr.MK is a normal chain — just press them in sequence
- From here, same DR BnB rules apply as the standalone cr.MK route above

**Common drops:**
- st.MP used at range where cr.MK won't link reliably
- All the same drop points as cr.MK DR BnB
