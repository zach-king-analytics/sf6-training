# Oki Setups

Post-knockdown positioning setups. The goal of each entry is to document the meaty timing
and which knock-down source creates each opportunity.

---

## Midscreen — After HP DP

### HP DP → Walk-up Meaty cr.LK

**Notation:** `HP DP → [walk forward] → cr.LK (meaty)`

| Resource cost | Position | Oki type | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|----------|---------|
| 0 bars | Midscreen | Meaty low | Not Started |

**Setup:** After landing HP DP mid-screen, walk forward during their knockdown animation and time cr.LK to land on the first active frame of their wake-up.

**Why cr.LK:** Low meaty — beats standing guard, stuffs reversal startup if timed correctly, plus on block.

**Beats:** Mash, block
**Loses to:** Late reversal DP with clean timing

**Key timing:**
- Walk forward immediately after HP DP lands
- Press cr.LK as the opponent begins to rise — not after they stand

## Corner — After HP DP

### HP DP (corner) → Dash-in Meaty cr.MK

**Notation:** `HP DP (corner) → [dash] → cr.MK (meaty)`

| Resource cost | Position | Oki type | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|----------|---------|
| 0 bars | Corner | Meaty mid | Not Started |

**Setup:** After landing HP DP with opponent in corner, dash in during their knockdown and time cr.MK as a meaty on wake-up.

**Why cr.MK:** Meaty cr.MK is +3 on block — gives enough advantage to follow with st.MP or throw mix. Cancellable into DR on hit for continued corner pressure.

**Beats:** Mash, block
**Loses to:** Reversal DP (needs bait read) / backdash (walk in and re-pressure)

**Key timing:**
- Dash immediately after HP DP — do not delay
- cr.MK timing is slightly lenient in corner due to less distance to cover
