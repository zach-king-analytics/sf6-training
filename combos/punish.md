# Punish Routes

Routes executed when the opponent commits an unsafe action.
Select the route based on available Drive gauge and screen position.

---

## Meterless

### cr.HP ~ HP DP

**Notation:** `cr.HP ~ HP DP`

| Resource cost | Position | Damage (approx) | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 0 bars | Any | ~1700 | Not Started |

**Starter:** cr.HP as a punish button on opponent's unsafe move (e.g. blocked DP, blocked super)

**Key timing:**
- HP DP cancel must be immediate — do not buffer slowly
- Works from any screen position

**Common drops:**
- Input dropped under pressure (hit confirm anxiety)
- HP DP fires too late and misses the juggle

## 2 Bars — OD Punish

### cr.HP ~ OD DP

**Notation:** `cr.HP ~ OD DP`

| Resource cost | Position | Damage (approx) | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 2 bars | Any | ~2100 | Not Started |

**Starter:** cr.HP punish, when you have 2 Overdrive bars to spend and no DR

**Key timing:**
- Same cancel timing as meterless — OD DP is more forgiving in juggle window

**Common drops:**
- Accidentally spending the OD on DR instead
- Wrong OD input (PP confusion with HP DP motion)

## 3 Bars — Drive Rush Punish

### cr.HP ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ HP DP

**Notation:** `cr.HP ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ HP DP`

| Resource cost | Position | Damage (approx) | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 3 bars | Midscreen | ~2800 | Not Started |

**Starter:** cr.HP punish with 3+ Drive bars, midscreen

**Key timing:**
- DR cancel off cr.HP is strict — must be buffered during the special cancel window
- st.MP link after DR: wait for the DR animation to finish, then press
- Second cr.HP links from st.MP, same as confirms route

**Common drops:**
- DR cancel too late off cr.HP
- st.MP links into the second cr.HP become inconsistent under pressure

## 5 Bars — Max Damage Punish

### cr.HP ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ OD Tatsu ~ HP DP

**Notation:** `cr.HP ~ DR ~ st.MP ~ cr.HP ~ OD Tatsu ~ HP DP`

| Resource cost | Position | Damage (approx) | Mastery |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 3 bars DR + 2 bars OD | Midscreen | ~3400 | Not Started |

**Starter:** cr.HP punish with full resources — use only when opponent is at or near burnout threshold

**Key timing:**
- Same opener as the 3-bar DR punish
- OD Tatsu replaces HP DP as the ender; HP DP follows as the juggle finisher
- The juggle window after OD Tatsu is strict — HP DP must be immediate

**Common drops:**
- HP DP after OD Tatsu drops (most common — timing is tight)
- Burning 5 bars unnecessarily when opponent is not near burnout
