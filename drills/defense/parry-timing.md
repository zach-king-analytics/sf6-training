# Drill: Parry Timing

- drill_id: parry-timing
- category: defense
- target_stage: In Drill

## Goal

Build controlled parry usage for predictable pressure points.

## Setup

- Dummy with repeatable strings
- Include throw option to avoid parry autopilot

## Reps

- 5 sets x 20 defensive reps

## Pass Criteria

- >= 70% successful parry timing on chosen strings
- No repeated parry spam patterns

## Failure Notes

- Early parry release
- Predictable defensive rhythm
