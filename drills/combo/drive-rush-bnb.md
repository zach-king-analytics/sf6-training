# Drill: Drive Rush BnB

- drill_id: drive-rush-bnb
- category: combo
- target_stage: Consistent

## Goal

Improve Drive Rush conversion reliability under realistic spacing.

## Setup

- Dummy: random block
- Start from common poke confirms
- Practice both midscreen and corner

## Reps

- 4 sets x 25 reps

## Pass Criteria

- >= 75% successful confirms
- No burnout panic sequences

## Failure Notes

- Spending drive before confirming
- Dropping juggle timing
