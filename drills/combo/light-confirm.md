# Drill: Light Confirm

- drill_id: light-confirm
- category: combo
- target_stage: Consistent

## Goal

Convert close-range light starters into stable confirms.

## Setup

- Dummy: random block on
- Range: close
- Route: your primary confirm sequence

## Reps

- 5 sets x 20 reps

## Pass Criteria

- >= 80% clean confirms over total reps

## Failure Notes

- Late confirm recognition
- Wrong cancel timing
